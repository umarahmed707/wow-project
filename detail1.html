WAT ARUN TEMPLE

description

Wat Arun is one of Bangkok's most important Buddhist temples. It's named after Aruna, the Hindu god of dawn, and its distinctive spires, or "prang" make it one of Thailand's most identifiable landmarks. At around 82 m (270 feet) in height, the central prang of the Wat Arun Temple is the highest in Bangkok.Wat Arun is one of Bangkok's most important Buddhist temples.
